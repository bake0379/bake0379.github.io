About Me

My name is Troy Joseph Knox Baker. I was named after my dad's father, Joseph Troy Baker. I was born abroad, in Paris, to a mother in the Canadian External Affairs, and a father in the American Air Force.We traveled around a bit, when I was very young. But we settled in Southern California before my 4th birthday. Here, Dad retired, and Mom got a job with the USACE, my brother was born, and we grew up into adulthood.

Growing up with stories of my parent's travels and adventures, I'd always wanted to leave and explore new parts of the world myself. On a vacation, my partner and I visited and fell in love with Portland, Oregon. We moved there withing six months, and even after the relationship dissolved, I stayed for years. Now I'm here in Ottawa, taking advantage of my barely-used Canadian citizenship. I hope to see even more of the world as time keeps marching on.
